# Goblin Scaling — Thumbnail Concepts

Five 1280×720 thumbnail mockups for the game. All composed from the in-repo
art (goblin sprite sheets + building renders) with gothic typography matching
the in-game New Rocker / VT323 / Cinzel mix.

| # | File | Concept | Notes |
|---|------|---------|-------|
| 1 | `1_hero_portrait.png` | **Hero portrait.** A single goblin looms in the centre, red rim-glow, gothic silhouettes of datacentres and a hypercentre in the background. | Goblin occupies ~50% of frame height — satisfies the "33%+" brief. |
| 2 | `2_army_horde.png`    | **Army horde.** Five rows of marching goblins (~57 total) under a blood sun, hypercentre tower behind, tagline *"the horde is hiring."* | Satisfies the "army of goblins" brief. |
| 3 | `3_goblin_wheel.png`  | **Goblin wheel.** Giant wheel with a goblin running inside, neon purple rack-wall of datacentres behind, tagline *"AI runs on goblins. You run the goblins."* | Leans into the labour-as-compute joke. |
| 4 | `4_cyber_gothic.png`  | **Cyber gothic.** Dynamic "swiping" goblin pose with cyan + magenta rim glows, glitched chromatic-aberration title, server racks behind, scan lines. | Most "thumbnail-y" / clicky of the bunch. |
| 5 | `5_skyline_sprawl.png` | **Skyline sprawl.** Sunset silhouettes of all the buildings (datacentre, hypercentre, nuclear reactor, gas engine, phone farm) with chunky goblins of varying sizes across the foreground. | "Scaling" motif — different-sized goblins. |

All compositions live in `/tmp/compose_thumbnails.py` (build script — not
committed; assets are the committed product).
